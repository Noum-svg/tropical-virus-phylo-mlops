About

Tropical Virus MLOps learns a tropical correction X = D + ω that reduces tropical four-point violations of a viral sequence distance matrix, then reconstructs a Neighbor-Joining tree.

Non-diagnostic research and educational tool. Reducing four-point violations improves mathematical compatibility with an additive tree metric; it does not establish biological correctness.
